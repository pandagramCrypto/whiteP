Pandagramm: Whitepaper

A Meme Coin with a Decentralized Vision

Introduction: A Meme Coin with a Decentralized Vision

Pandagramm is more than just a meme coin. This innovative project aims to redefine the social media experience by integrating a decentralized blockchain (based on Polygon) and allowing its users not only to earn rewards for their engagement but also to actively participate in the governance of the project. Pandagramm seeks to merge meme culture and trending social media movements with the benefits of a sustainable and community-driven digital economy.

Pandagramm’s Mission

Our mission is to create a platform where users can interact, create content, and be rewarded for their engagement, while also having real power over the evolution of the project. We aim to build an ecosystem where every action counts and where the community actively shapes the future of Pandagramm.

Features and Community Collaboration

Pandagramm is committed to offering a unique platform that combines both the power of decentralized technology and the engagement of social networking. The core features of the platform include content creation, user interaction, and rewards for engagement, all powered by the PandagrammCoin (PGM). Users will be able to post content, participate in social activities, and interact with others, creating a vibrant community environment. This interaction will be rewarded with tokens, incentivizing continuous engagement. In addition, Pandagramm's decentralized governance allows users to propose and vote on new features, improvements, and the strategic direction of the platform. The success of Pandagramm hinges on active community participation, and every decision made on the platform will be a result of collective input. By combining the social aspects of networking with a transparent, blockchain-based governance model, Pandagramm empowers its users to shape the platform, making it a true partnership between the project and its community.

Technology and Economy of Pandagramm

Pandagramm relies on a decentralized blockchain built on Polygon, a scaling solution for the Ethereum blockchain. This decision allows us to significantly reduce transaction fees while ensuring increased scalability and speed.

The Token: PandagrammCoin (PGM)

The native token of Pandagramm is PandagrammCoin (PGM), an ERC-20 token integrated into the Polygon blockchain for fast and cost-effective transactions. PGM will be used to reward users for their engagement, participate in governance, and facilitate transactions within the ecosystem.

Technical Features of the Token:

  • Token Name: PandagrammCoin
  • Symbol: PGM
  • Token Standard: ERC-20 (on Polygon blockchain)
  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 (1 billion) PGM tokens
  • Type: Used for user rewards, governance, and transactions within the ecosystem.
  • Initial Distribution Period: December 28th to 31st, 2024, for the first airdrop.

Token Distribution and Allocation

The distribution of the 1 billion PGM tokens is designed to ensure fair allocation, incentivize engagement, and provide sustainable funding for the development of the Pandagramm ecosystem.

Token Allocation:

  • Airdrop (10%) – 100,000,000 PGM - Free token distribution to users to encourage sign-ups, participation, and active community creation.
  • Engagement Rewards (20%) – 200,000,000 PGM - Tokens rewarded daily based on user engagement.
  • Development and Ecosystem (30%) – 300,000,000 PGM - Funding for ongoing platform development, dApps, and feature integration.
  • Governance and Reserves (15%) – 150,000,000 PGM - Tokens for platform governance and future reserves.
  • Partnerships and Marketing (10%) – 100,000,000 PGM - Funding for marketing and strategic partnerships.
  • Team and Advisors (15%) – 150,000,000 PGM - Compensations for the founding team, developers, and advisors with a vesting period.

Development and Ecosystem (30%) – 300,000,000 PGM

30% of the total PandagrammCoin (PGM) supply, amounting to 300,000,000 PGM tokens, will be dedicated to the ongoing development and expansion of the Pandagramm platform and its ecosystem. This allocation will fund the continuous improvement of the social network itself, ensuring that it remains engaging, secure, and scalable for its growing community. Additionally, these funds will support the development of new applications within the ecosystem, enhancing the platform’s capabilities and offering more value to users. Part of this allocation will also be used to finance partner applications, helping to foster collaboration and integration with other projects that align with Pandagramm’s vision. By investing in the growth of both the social network and partner ecosystem, we aim to create a robust, sustainable platform that can support a wide variety of use cases and ensure long-term success for the entire community.

Governance Mechanism and Participation

Pandagramm adopts a decentralized governance model where every PGM token holder has a voice in the platform’s evolution. Governance is based on a proportional voting system, where each user can propose and vote on new features, protocol changes, or strategic decisions.

Voting System:

  • Proposals: PGM holders can submit proposals regarding the platform’s evolution. These proposals are then put to a community vote.
  • Vote Weight: The weight of a vote is directly related to the number of PGM tokens held by the user.
  • Goal: Provide every user with a true influence on decisions regarding the project’s future.

Pandagramm's First Airdrop

We are excited to announce the first Pandagramm airdrop! This event will allow participants to receive free PandagrammCoin (PGM) tokens as part of our community-building effort. The airdrop will run from December 28th to 31st, 2024, during which 1% of the total supply of PGM tokens will be distributed to early participants. A total of 10,000,000 PGM tokens will be available for this airdrop, and distribution will happen daily over the course of the event.

How to Participate

To participate in the airdrop and receive your free tokens, please follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Submit your wallet address to us via email or Telegram. You can send your address to airdrop@pandagramm.com or directly message us on our official Telegram channel.
  • Step 2: Ensure that you provide a valid Polygon-compatible wallet address (ERC-20) to receive your PGM tokens.
  • Step 3: After submitting your wallet address, you will receive a portion of the airdropped tokens each day during the event period, up to 1% of the total supply (100,000,000 PGM tokens in total).

Be sure to register quickly, as this is a limited-time event and spots are available on a first-come, first-served basis. We look forward to having you join the Pandagramm community!
